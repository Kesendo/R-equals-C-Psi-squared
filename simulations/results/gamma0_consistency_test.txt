8) Verdict
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Best-case CV across interpretations: 42.6% (interp=A_max_mode)
  Best-case max/min ratio: 3.31x (interp=A_max_mode)

  Success criterion (task):   max/min < 3x across non-outlier qubits
  Failure criterion (task):   max/min > 10x after outlier removal
  Sub-chain observed max/min: 3.31x

  VERDICT: INCONCLUSIVE: falls between success and failure thresholds.

  Caveat: this test uses idle-Ramsey gamma_phi against gate-active J.
  Idle dynamics has H_idle ~ diagonal (no chain mode structure), so the
  per-qubit T2 directly measures local dephasing, NOT chain-mode dephasing.
  The mode-exposure formula applies to a chain running its coupling
  Hamiltonian (e.g. during a CPsi crossing experiment). A proper test
  requires Phase 2: run chain dynamics on the selected sub-chain and
  compare observed decoherence to gamma_0 * |a_B|^2.

  EQ-017 reading (b) is easier to test from idle data alone:
  the minimum gamma_phi across non-outlier qubits gives a gamma_0 floor
  candidate of 0.000730 /us, corresponding to T2_framework = 2739.2 us.
