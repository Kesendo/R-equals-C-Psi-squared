===========================================================================
SUMMARY
===========================================================================

1. REFLECTION/TRANSMISSION: The sacrifice zone increases the oscillating
   fraction and Q-factor at every N. It is a better-matched cavity.

2. IMPEDANCE MATCHING: gamma_edge ~ N*gamma_base (linear, not geometric
   mean). The sacrifice zone is a linear accumulator, not a classical
   AR coating. But it achieves the same effect: smooth entry of light.

3. MODE-SELECTIVE: The sacrifice zone preserves oscillation FREQUENCIES
   while dramatically changing DECAY RATES. Structurally identical to
   an AR coating that changes reflectivity without moving resonances.

4. TRANSFER MATRIX: Frequencies unchanged, decay rates shifted.
   The sacrifice zone acts on Re(lambda) only, leaving Im(lambda)
   invariant. This is the entrance pupil / AR coating mechanism.

5. SCALING: Quadratic (N^2), not exponential. The cavity is dispersive,
   not a simple dielectric stack.

VERDICT: The sacrifice zone IS the entrance pupil of the cavity.
Structural analog to AR coating confirmed. Not quantitatively identical
(linear scaling vs geometric mean), but functionally equivalent.
