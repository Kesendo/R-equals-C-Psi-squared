   From 0 (N=1) to ~15 (N=5) distinct frequencies.

   Regime C (Zundel, J/γ=5): high Q, coherent oscillation.

   Same geometric mechanism as qubit chains and DNA.

   The equation is universal; the parameters are substrate-specific.
