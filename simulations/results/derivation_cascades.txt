CASCADE A: Q-factor distribution
  From formula 2: Q_k = 2J/g * (1 - cos(pi*k/N))
  Prediction: arcsine distribution between Q_min, Q_max
============================================================

  Analytical derivation:
    Q_k = 2J/g * (1 - cos(pi*k/N)),  k=1..N-1
    For large N, k/N -> continuous variable q in (0,1)
    Q(q) = 2J/g * (1 - cos(pi*q))
    dQ/dq = 2J*pi/g * sin(pi*q)
    rho(Q) = |dq/dQ| = g/(2J*pi) / sin(pi*q)
           = 1 / (pi * sqrt(Q * (4J/g - Q)))
    This is the arcsine distribution on [Q_min, Q_max].
