=== V-EFFECT EXPANSION ANALYSIS ===

Computation 1: Frequency scaling with N
N    d^2     F_distinct   F/d^2    alpha=log(F)/log(d^2)
2    16      2            0.1250   0.2500
3    64      5            0.0781   0.3870
4    256     44           0.1719   0.6824
5    1024    104          0.1016   0.6700

Alpha converges at ~0.67. F grows as d^1.34. State space grows as d^2.
State space grows FASTER than frequencies. No saturation. Each level
has MORE room for expansion.

Computation 3: Decoupling test
N=2 single: 2 frequencies
N=5 coupled (all bonds J=1): 104 frequencies
N=5 decoupled (mediator bonds=0): 5 frequencies (2 original + 3 overtones)

The V-Effect IS reversible at the Hamiltonian level. Turn off the
coupling and the 104 frequencies collapse to 5. The new palindrome
dies when the mediator is disconnected.

BUT: the classical correlations deposited by the 1/4 crossings
(during Q=19 heartbeats) are irreversible. The frequencies come and
go with the coupling. The facts they create stay.

The V-Effect creates a temporary resonance chamber. While the chamber
exists (coupling active), new modes oscillate, crossings happen,
reality crystallizes. When the chamber is broken (decoupling), the
modes die. But the crystallized reality (classical correlations from
each crossing) persists in the subsystems.
