Algebraic obstruction: the 4×4 char poly det(λI − L_eff(Q)) is a genuine
quartic in (λ, Q), NOT a biquadratic. Sampled coefficients show:
  c_0(Q) = 1
  c_1(Q) = 16γ₀ − i·Q·γ₀·trace(M_h_total_eff)        (linear in Q)
  c_2(Q) = 16γ₀² + a·Q²                              (quadratic in Q)
  c_3(Q) = (γ₀-terms) + b·Q² + c·Q³                  (CUBIC in Q ⇒ no biquad)
  c_4(Q) = (γ₀⁴-terms) + d·Q² + e·Q⁴                 (quartic in Q)
The cubic term in c_3 rules out factorisation into
(λ² − aλ + b)(λ² − cλ + d) with closed-form rational coefficients in Q.
