## §1 Definitions and the statement

All objects are F133's (definitions verbatim in [PROOF_F133_W_SYMPLECTIC_CLOSED_FORM §§1, 3](PROOF_F133_W_SYMPLECTIC_CLOSED_FORM.md)): the 143 integer coefficients n_λ of K = 2⁻³⁰ Σ_λ n_λ χ^{C₆}_λ, indexed by partitions λ with at most 6 parts, even |λ| ≤ 18, and λ₁ + λ₂ ≤ 10 (the wall); ρ = (6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1) and μ = λ + ρ; the read-off n_raw(λ) = Σ_{ε∈{±1}⁶} sgn(ε)·[X]_{ε∘2(λ+ρ)} = 2 n_λ on X = Δ̂·∏₃₁ (the deleted-top-sheet spinor product times the half-angle Vandermonde). Entries outside the committed table are 0. A **two-row** λ is (j, k, 0, 0, 0, 0) with j ≥ k ≥ 0, written n_(j,k).

**Theorem (F134, the two-row reflection law).** For every two-row partition in the support,

  **n_(j,k) = n_(10−j,k)**  for all 0 ≤ k ≤ j ≤ 10 − k.

Equivalently: in μ = λ + ρ coordinates the reflection is **μ₁ ↦ 22 − μ₁** with every other coordinate fixed; equivalently, each column polynomial C_k(q) = Σ_j n_(j,k) q^j is palindromic about q⁵.

**Grade.** The statement is finite: 36 pairs, 14 of them with nonzero entries. It is verified exactly over ℤ by the committed gate ([simulations/f134_two_row_reflection_law.py](../../simulations/f134_two_row_reflection_law.py), G1) through the committed F133 read-off, and independently recomputed in C# (§6). As with the F127 wall, the caveat is code trust only; no analytic step is involved in the statement's verification. What is NOT proved is a structural mechanism; §5 is the honest map of that opening.

## §2 The proven structure around the law

Write F_k(m) for the W(C₅)-antisymmetrized first-coordinate slice of X at the frozen tail (2k+10, 8, 6, 4, 2), m = 2μ₁, so that F_k(2j+12) = n_raw((j,k)) on the window m ∈ [12+2k, 32−2k] (gate G1/G2 machinery; this is the object the law lives on).

**(a) The finite half is a theorem.** F_k(−m) = −F_k(m) identically: total negation multiplies each of the 46 antisymmetric factors of X by −1, and (−1)⁴⁶ = +1 makes the coefficient function even, while the read-off's sorting sign is odd. Gate G2; proved algebraically in the arc via the full-complementation involution.

**(b) The wall is a theorem.** F_k(m) = 0 for |m| > 32−2k: X's per-variable exponents lie in [−36, 36], forced by the factor count (F133 gate 5).

**(c) The Θ-decomposition.** Define the explicit Laurent polynomials (here C_a := t^a + t^{−a}, the Chebyshev shorthand, not §1's column polynomial C_k(q))

  P₀ = −(C₁₀ + C₆), P₁ = (t⁴ − t⁻⁴)², P₂ = 3C₂, P₃ = −(C₄ + 3), P₄ = C₂, P₅ = 0,

and **Θ_k := (t²² − t⁻²²)·P_k**. Then the full coefficient array of F_k equals Θ_k plus a defect supported strictly outside the window (gate G3), and Θ_k is exactly one period of an honest dihedral object: its anti-periodic lift from the fundamental domain [−21, 22] reproduces Θ_k on the whole support range and is odd about 0 and even about 22 globally (gate G3). In words: **the array is a theta-with-characteristic minus its beyond-wall shadow**, both parts explicit; every defect point's reflection partner lies past the proven wall.

**(d) Equivalences.** Given (a), the law is equivalent to the in-window anti-period F_k(m+44) = −F_k(m), and to the confinement of the defect in (c) to the off-window gap. These are restatements, not mechanisms; each has been verified independently.

## §3 The affine level dictionary

In the standard untwisted C₆⁽¹⁾ dictionary (θ = 2ε₁, θ∨ = ε₁, ⟨μ, θ∨⟩ = μ₁, dual Coxeter number h∨ = 7, translation lattice ν(Q∨) = 2ℤ⁶): the reflection wall μ₁ = 11 = ℓ + h∨ and the translation step 22 = 2(ℓ + h∨) simultaneously force **shifted level 11, level ℓ = 4**. ## §4 The domain

The law is exact on the two-row slice and does not extend freely. Measured exactly (gate G5): with a third row l, the reflection holds cleanly at l ∈ {0, 1, 3} (and at l = 4, 5), and breaks at l = 2 on exactly 8 pairs, (j,k) ∈ {(2,2), (3,3), (4,2), (4,4), (6,2), (6,4), (7,3), (8,2)}; the two nonzero l = 2 entries that do satisfy the reflection are the center-fixed weights j = 5 (μ₁ = 11, mapped to themselves). The naive reading "l = 2 is the resonant value" is a projection artifact: beyond three rows the correct invariant is the s₀-fixed tail (μ₂, …, μ₆), and the complete tail inventory splits the breaks into a support-truncation tier (implied by the wall, no false positives), one support-gap case, and a residue of pure coefficient asymmetries carrying no coordinate signature at all. That residue is the sharpest constraint any future mechanism must reproduce.

## §5 What the mechanism is not: the closed routes

The structural identity performing s₀ remains open. The hunt closed the internal routes with small arguments rather than failed searches; they are recorded here as the map, so no future session reopens them. Of the six, the centroid obstruction is the one gated in the committed verification (G6); the others were established in the arc's working layer and are stated at that grade:

- **No constituent cancellation at any depth**: the read-off's atomic incidence structure is block-diagonal (64 distinct targets, no shared sub-object), so the vanishing cannot factor through the read-off's own combinatorics. The k = 4 instance of the law telescopes to the single table equality n_(4,4) = n_(6,4) = 1, whose 32-term flip-sheet expansion has integers to ±305 with no pairing: one equal pair of table cells, computed 32 ways.
- **No affine array symmetry**: X's coefficient function satisfies Xc(−v) = Xc(v) exactly (gate G6), so its support centroid is 0 and every affine symmetry has translation part 0; the full affine symmetry group is the linear S₆ × ℤ₂. The center 22 is off the centroid: the law can only ever be a window identity.
- **No finite-Weyl or modification-rule origin**: the Koike-Terada/King modification rules are finite W(C₆) operations and deliver exactly §2a, never the affine pivot.
- **No telescope or boundary sum**: the deleted-sheet geometric-series ladder's relations are all generated by §2a; folding the reflection through them is circular.
- **No term bijection**: reflected partners have different flip-ladder lengths with equal sums; the identity is intrinsically summed.
- **Not a twisted-affine denominator**: the A₁₂⁽²⁾/BC₆ Macdonald denominator contains only rank ≤ 2 factors, no spinor factor to delete; yet that family is the unique affine genre producing the (−, +) character on W(C₆), which is why the genre diagnosis stands while the identity match fails.

The open problem, cleanly posed: exhibit the identity (theta/affine-character grade, on the Langlands seam between the B₆ spin weight system and the C₆ character lattice) that makes §1 a corollary, reproduces the §4 break inventory, and specializes to the (−, +) characteristic of §3.
