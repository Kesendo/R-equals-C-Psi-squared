## What this document is about

The translation series gave the label thesis a theory chapter and two case
studies, and the theory chapter makes a claim about this repository itself:
"our own review history says the same thing at repository scale." This map is
that claim, itemized. We surveyed the whole repository for every place where
a name was found wrong while the mathematics under it held, and assembled the
results in one place, so that we can orient ourselves: see the pattern as a
pattern, find each precedent when we need it, and know which label problems
are still open.

The consistent finding, across every entry below: the underlying object (a
spectrum, an operator, a closed form, a hardware number) was left untouched;
the correction landed on the name, the attribution, or the reading. The one
partial exception is marked where it occurs.

## 1. The thesis and its theory (where the ideas live)

- **The thesis:** the world's calculations are watertight; the labels are the
  error surface. A calculation is recomputed at every use, so its errors die
  young; a label is inherited at every use and recomputed at none, so errors
  accumulate exactly where nothing recomputes.
  Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §1.
- **The grounding:** a label is a canvas, painted true at a stance (a vantage
  at a time); the failure mode is stance-forgetting, a canvas inherited raw
  into a stance it was not painted from. Grounded in the
  [Perspectival Time Field](../../hypotheses/PERSPECTIVAL_TIME_FIELD.md)
  (Tier 2 computed): no canvas is privileged, and there is no tenth canvas.
  Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §3.
- **The error signal:** labels fail silently in the calculation channel; the
  label layer's one error signal is a displaced reader's felt wrongness, the
  complaint, and PTF explains why it arrives generations late.
  Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §2.
- **The t-axis:** hold the physics fixed and slide only t; if a sentence's
  truth value moves, it was never a sentence about the physics. The abstract
  component of a label transports; the indexical component fossilizes.
  Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §3.
- **The flip:** the formulas are read-only; the labels are the one writable
  layer the world hands its observers. All the error lives there because all
  the agency lives there. Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md),
  the closing section.
- **Whose layer it is:** not the human layer, the observer layer. Even the
  environment routes by a label (local Z-dephasing reads exactly one letter
  and charges −2γk for disagreement in that letter only).
  Owner: [Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §2. Typed 2026-07-05:
  `WatchedLetterRoutingClaim` (Tier 1 derived, parents Absorption + Klein V₄),
  recomputed live at `inspect --root label`.

## 5. The open label seams (live, unresolved)

- **C is triply overloaded** (coupling scalar, concurrence, legacy
  "Consciousness"), and Ψ, CΨ, R similarly. The
  [Glossary](../GLOSSARY.md) states the artifact once but does not rename;
  a standing ambiguity.
- **q vs Q keeps re-biting.** Documented in the Glossary, yet it recurred as
  a fresh catch on 2026-06-27; reconciled in prose, enforced nowhere in
  notation. Expect recurrence.
- **"Center" is ambiguous at odd N** (the chain/sector center splits across
  two positions, weakening center-dependent scores); flagged in
  [Bures Degeneracy](../../experiments/BURES_DEGENERACY.md) and
  [Thermal Breaking](../../experiments/THERMAL_BREAKING.md), not resolved.
- **Π's temporal language** ("past→future") is deliberately tier-split
  (algebra Tier 2, temporal language Tier 3, philosophical reading Tier 5)
  in [The Interpretation](../THE_INTERPRETATION.md); an acknowledged seam
  left open by design.
- **EQ-014's level question:** why Σ f_i is small for bonding-mode states
  but order-unity for |+⟩^⊗7 is "a pattern in the data, not (yet) a
  theorem" ([Emerging Questions](../../review/EMERGING_QUESTIONS.md)); the
  right name and level for that coefficient are unsettled.

## 6. The discipline (how the next label runs)

- **The practical rule:** when something is incomprehensible, diff the label
  before doubting the math ([Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §5).
- **The naming-note protocol** (the PTF precedent): rename, do not archive;
  keep every number; write a dated naming note that defends each word of the
  new name; never revert on grounds of familiarity
  ([Perspectival Time Field](../../hypotheses/PERSPECTIVAL_TIME_FIELD.md),
  Naming note).
- **The closure operation:** a translation is not the tenth canvas; lay the
  popular label with its native stance, the untouched algebra, and our
  reading side by side, and show they close over one object
  ([Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §5).
- **The timestamp:** our recomputed labels are canvases stamped t = 2026 and
  mortal; a naming note carries its date so the future reader receives the
  canvas with its t instead of raw
  ([Labels Translated](LABELS_TRANSLATED.md) §3).
- **The logs that hold the cases:** [Caught Errors](../CAUGHT_ERRORS.md)
  (chronological, append-only), `recovered/` (premature rather than wrong),
  [The Interpretation](../THE_INTERPRETATION.md) ("What Fell").

To extend this map: when the next label falls, add one row to the matching
section, cite the document that owns the correction, and leave the numbers
where they are.
