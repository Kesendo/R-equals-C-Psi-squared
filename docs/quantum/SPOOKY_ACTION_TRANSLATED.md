## What this document is about

This is the oldest debt on the map. Since the day the
[Label Map](THE_LABEL_MAP.md) was drawn, its "spooky action at a distance"
row has pointed at a legacy document with the note "still in the deprecated
framing, kept as history". Every other row of that table got its
translation; this one waited. And the label does not wait with it: it
arrives bundled with the cat in nearly every video, the two particles that
"communicate instantly across the universe", the measurement here that
"changes reality there". The sixth entry noticed that the cat and
Verschränkung share a birth certificate; this entry reads the certificate.

The discipline is the series' usual one, perspective-additive: the
standard account needs no correction from us, and gets none. Both of its
theorems stand exactly as proven, and this entry's whole work is to hold
the two of them up side by side: the correlations are real and provably
exceed every local story, and nothing is ever sent. The excess is a
theorem. The action is the fossil.

## 1. The record, stated plainly

An entangled pair |Φ⁺⟩ = (|00⟩ + |11⟩)/√2 is created in one place and
carried apart at ordinary speed. Alice measures her half: a fair coin.
Bob measures his: a fair coin. Compared later, over an ordinary phone
line, the coins match. So far, nothing spooky: mismatched socks in two
mailed parcels would do the same, and Bell himself made exactly that
joke (Bertlmann's socks, 1981): see one sock pink, know the other is
not, and nobody calls the mail spooky.

Two theorems frame everything else.

**The excess is real.** Bell (1964) turned the philosophical dispute into
an inequality, and its CHSH form (Clauser, Horne, Shimony and Holt,
1969) into a score: any story in which the outcomes were fixed in
advance by shared local instructions, however elaborate, keeps the
score at or below 2. The quantum pair scores up to 2√2 (the Tsirelson
bound), and the experiments confirm it: Freedman and Clauser (1972),
Aspect (1982), the loophole-free tests of 2015, the 2022 Nobel Prize.
The correlations of the pair provably exceed what any sock drawer can
produce. That is not a pop exaggeration; it is the hardest-tested
theorem in the foundations of physics.

**Nothing is sent.** The no-communication theorem, the standard account's
own lock: nothing Alice does, measurement, unitary, anything, changes
Bob's local statistics by any amount. His marginal state is I/2 before,
during, and after, whatever happens in her lab. No message, no energy,
no bias, no timing signal ever crosses. Every Bell experiment above is
also, implicitly, a confirmation of this: the correlations only appear
when the two records are brought together, over a classical channel, at
light speed or slower.

The popular story keeps the first theorem, drops the second, and welds
the remainder into a sentence about influence: measuring one particle
*instantly affects* the other. Both halves of that sentence are wrong,
and each is wrong against one of the two theorems.

## 2. The native stance: an objection, for the third time

Einstein's phrase is from a letter to Max Born (1947): he could not take
quantum mechanics as complete, because taken as complete it seemed to
entail "spukhafte Fernwirkungen", spooky actions at a distance. The
structure of the sentence matters more than its famous adjective: the
spook lives inside a conditional, *if* the wavefunction is the whole
description, *then* measurement here does something there, and that is
absurd, *therefore* the description is incomplete. The EPR paper (1935)
runs the same argument: "no reasonable definition of reality could be
expected to permit this." The phrase was painted as a reductio, an
argument that something was missing.

This is the third time this series has met that exact shape, and it is
worth saying so: EPR's spook, Schrödinger's cat, and the smeared Ψ were
all painted in the same 1935 debate, all as objections, and all three
were transported into the popular canon with the sign flipped, complaints
retold as features. Intent-forgetting, three times over.

But this label's resolution differs from the cat's. The cat's complaint
eventually got its update (decoherence, the price list). Einstein's got
a verdict instead. Bell's 1964 inequality made the dispute experimental,
and the experiments ruled on both clauses at once: the unease was
pointing at something real, these correlations do exceed every local
classical story, though Einstein himself expected a local completion to
absorb them; and the remedy he wanted, local hidden instructions, is
precisely what the theorem forbids. The "action" clause failed
separately and completely: no-communication. The stance smelled
something real and filed it under the only genre 1947 had for it,
influence. What froze was the word "action". The excess it pointed at
has a Nobel Prize; the action was never there.

## 3. Where the label breaks

**"Action."** Nothing acts. Bob's marginal is I/2 under everything Alice
can do; that is the standard account's theorem, and this repository has
run its own version at machine precision: a projective measurement on
one half of a Bell pair, averaged over its unread outcomes, moves the
pair's joint CΨ from 0.500 to 0.250 while the other half's reduced
state does not move at all, ‖Δρ_A‖ = 0 exactly
([No-Signalling Boundary](../../experiments/NO_SIGNALLING_BOUNDARY.md),
Tier 2). (A convention note, carried from that source: its 0.500 reads
C as purity and Ψ as the marginal's top eigenvalue, while Section 4's
1/3 is the lens convention, concurrence times normalized coherence.
One pair, two bookkeepings; the overloaded C is the first seam on the
Label Map's open list, and this collision is its sharpest instance.)
Something real changed, in the joint books; nothing changed on the
local page. That is the signature of an accounting fact, not of a
force.

**"At a distance."** The word imports space as the relevant separation.
But the correlation was paid for locally, when the pair was created, and
carried apart at ordinary speed, the pre-paid resource of the
[teleportation entry](TELEPORTATION_TRANSLATED.md); at measurement time
nothing crosses the gap because nothing needs to. What the label calls a
distance, the algebra holds as a shared matrix entry that belongs to no
single lab. This repository wrote the intuition down long before it had
the pieces, in a late-night aside it honestly labeled unworked: "neither
action nor distance. It is topology"
([Gamma Is Light](../../hypotheses/GAMMA_IS_LIGHT.md), the open thread).
This entry is that itch, worked.

**"The other particle changes."** Conditioning, not causation, and this
is now the third appearance of the same move in the series: the eraser
did not rewrite the past, it sorted the books; opening the box did not
decide the cat, it updated the opener; and Alice's outcome does not
touch Bob's particle, it updates *her conditional page about Bob*. His
own page never turns. When the two of them meet and compare, over
coffee and a classical channel, the books close, exactly as correlated
as the excess theorem demands and exactly as silent as the
no-communication theorem requires.

One precision, owed to the lens's own discipline: what survives,
perspective-bound, is the excess itself. These correlations really
cannot be explained by any local pre-agreement; that is not folklore,
it is the content of Bell's theorem and half a century of experiments.
Kill the action, keep the excess. The spook was a true perception filed
under the wrong genre.

## 4. The translation (the exact part)

**The carrier is one matrix entry, and we know its price.** Write the
pair out:

    |Φ⁺⟩⟨Φ⁺| = ½ ( |00⟩⟨00| + |11⟩⟨11| + |00⟩⟨11| + |11⟩⟨00| ).

Drop the last two entries and what remains is the sock drawer: the
classical mixture of 00 and 11, with identical marginals and identical
readouts in the basis the state is written in; every rotated shared
question tells the two apart. Everything the Bell experiments detect
beyond socks rides on |00⟩⟨11|: one off-diagonal coherence, disagreement
k = 2, the N = 2 case of the very object the cat entry priced, an exact
eigenmode decaying at −2(γ₁+γ₂)
([Absorption Theorem](../proofs/PROOF_ABSORPTION_THEOREM.md), Tier 1,
residual 0.0). The socks are the diagonal; the spook is the
off-diagonal; and the watching prices exactly the spook while leaving
the socks immortal, which is the algebra under the folk knowledge that
entanglement is fragile and classical correlation is not. The series'
one law, third sighting: k = 1 is the pattern, k = 2 is the pair,
k = N is the cat.

**Nothing is sent, in our own laboratory dialect.** Beside the standard
theorem, the repository's own Tier 2 witnesses:
[No-Signalling Boundary](../../experiments/NO_SIGNALLING_BOUNDARY.md)
(the ‖Δρ_A‖ = 0 measurement above) and
[Bridge Closure](../../experiments/BRIDGE_CLOSURE.md) (ρ_A = I/2 at all
times regardless of what is done to B). And in-series, the Klein
average of the [teleportation entry](TELEPORTATION_TRANSLATED.md):
a state whose frame label has not arrived reads as I/2, total
forgetting. Correlation without the classical channel is not a faint
signal; it is exactly no signal, and the two classical bits are what
rescue the state from its own average.

**Where the pair sits in our units.** The Bell pair stands at CΨ = 1/3
(concurrence 1 × normalized coherence 1/3, the
[glossary](../GLOSSARY.md) boundary table), above the ¼ boundary, and
local dephasing drags it down through ¼, the Layer 2 crossing of the
quarter-boundary roadmap, restated in the
[block-CΨ quarter proof](../proofs/PROOF_BLOCK_CPSI_QUARTER.md) (the
¼ bound itself is Tier 1; what physical transition ¼ marks is a question
the repository keeps explicitly open). The excess is not an ambient
glow; it is a budgeted quantity with a location, a price, and a decay
curve.

**The correlation lives in the joint books, and when it moves, it
walks.**
Stated as computed fact in the star-topology work: "the entanglement
information lives in the correlations, not in S alone"
([Star Topology](../../experiments/STAR_TOPOLOGY_OBSERVERS.md)). And
when correlation has to get somewhere in continuous dynamics, this
repository has watched how: hop by hop along bonds, stage by stage in
time, +83% end-to-end mutual information on an 11-qubit chain with no
measurements and no classical channel
([Relay Protocol](../../experiments/RELAY_PROTOCOL.md), computationally
verified at N = 11, C# RK4). Nothing in this repository has ever seen
correlation jump. It has watched it walk, and it has priced what it costs to
stand still.

## 6. An honest note on our own house

The house committed this label's error once itself, in reverse. For a
while our own mirror Π was filed as non-local (an operator Schmidt rank
of 8-9, the floor of a non-convex search), and the correction, when it
came, is the whole lesson of this entry in one sentence: "the
non-locality lived in the choice of representative, not in the symmetry"
([Π Operator Entanglement](../../experiments/PI_OPERATOR_ENTANGLEMENT.md);
the [Label Map](THE_LABEL_MAP.md) tallies some sixteen documents
corrected). Non-locality is easy to see and hard to have.

Our newcomer layer climbs through the pop gloss here as everywhere: the
[glossary](../GLOSSARY.md) explains concurrence with "what happens to
one instantly affects the other", which is precisely the sentence this
entry translates. The ladder stays; this document is where it is kicked
away.

Two scope boundaries, so the translation does not overreach. This
repository has never derived a Bell violation; Section 1's excess
theorem is cited as the standard account's own, and our exact
contributions are the carrier's price and the no-signalling witness.
And our "observer-dependent visibility" results
([Observer-Dependent Visibility](../../experiments/OBSERVER_DEPENDENT_VISIBILITY.md))
are noise-rate windows on a persistent resource, not the relativistic
"observer-dependent entanglement" of the literature, and no-signalling
holds throughout them; that document carries the same disclaimer.

## 7. The in-repo cousin

The [Relay Protocol](../../experiments/RELAY_PROTOCOL.md) is what
correlation transport looks like when it is real: relay stations along
an 11-qubit chain take turns listening, each stage clocked to the
watching, and end-to-end mutual information arrives at +83% over
passive propagation, with no resets, no measurements, no classical
communication. The [teleportation entry](TELEPORTATION_TRANSLATED.md)
already wrote the shared punchline once: in both protocols the thing
that travels is correlation, and correlation travels along structure,
not through space as a payload. The spooky-action label is what that
fact sounds like when the structure is forgotten and only the two
endpoints remain in view: take away the bonds, the shared origin, the
pre-paid entry, and a correlation with no visible path reads as a
reach across the void. Restore the structure and the void was never
crossed, because it was never empty.

## The right label

The excess, not the action. The pair: one shared coherence, k = 2,
priced like every coherence in this repository, sitting at CΨ = 1/3
until the watching collects. The "influence": a conditional page
turning in one lab's books while the other lab's page holds still at
machine precision. The "distance": unbridged, because nothing needed
to cross. Einstein's spook: a true smell filed under the wrong genre,
vindicated as excess by the very line of experiments that buried it as
action. And the sentence this entry compresses to: **the spook is
real, and it is not an action; it is how much more the between holds
than any two pages can.**

Stamped: this canvas is ours, painted 2026, and its mortal components
are already visible from here. "Excess" is measured against the
classical story, and some later stance may not keep "classical" as its
reference floor; and "the between", left raw, could freeze into a new
ether, a substance where the algebra holds only a shared entry. The
future reader receives the canvas with its date.

The closure, then: Einstein's canvas, an objection whose adjective was
a correct perception; Bell's canvas, the objection turned into an
inequality and the inequality into a verdict; the untouched algebra,
one off-diagonal entry, priced at −2(γ₁+γ₂), marginals locked at I/2;
the house's first canvas, kept as history with its fallen threads
marked; and our canvas, the excess. One pair. Nothing was ever sent,
nothing was ever spooky but the bookkeeping, and the bookkeeping was
telling the truth: there is more in the between than either side can
hold alone.
