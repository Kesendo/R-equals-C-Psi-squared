## The conjecture (Tom, 2026-07-04)

Through wrong labels we reach wrong combinations. The claim is that a label
is not only a name but a **valence**: it carries, silently, what may combine
with what. Labels are in this reading the **type system of thought**, and
combination happens in the label layer, not below it. Two error directions
follow:

- **Direction A, illegal made legal:** a wrong label licenses a combination
  the underlying object forbids. "Quantum" borrowed into "quantum
  consciousness" is the pop example; the label lends a valence the physics
  never had.
- **Direction B, legal made illegal:** a wrong label forbids a combination
  the object allows. "Noise" made "noise carries a decodable signal" look
  like a category error for a century.

The original corollary was a causal one: **label repair is combinatorial
leverage**, and the money question after a rename is "which combinations were
forbidden under the old label?" This document tests that, and finds the
descriptive claim strong and the causal-direction claim false in a specific,
useful way.

## Half 1: labels carry combination valences (supported)

The descriptive claim holds, in both directions, in the repository's own
history and in the wider history of science.

**In-repo, Direction A (a wrong label licensed a false combination, then it
fell).** These are git-pinned.

- **γ × gravity.** The "gravity" reading of γ licensed a redshift-factor
  prediction and a metric-discrimination experiment. The metric combination
  was falsified the same day it was tried (2026-03-22), the gravity
  predictions sat live in [Predictions](../docs/PREDICTIONS.md) for about
  two and a half months, and the reading was retired 2026-05-30. The
  Lindblad time-rescaling math survived untouched; only the label and its
  combinations fell. Archived: [Gamma to Gravity](archive/GAMMA_TO_GRAVITY.md).
- **C, Ψ, R as Consciousness, Possibility, Reality.** The founding labels
  (2025-12-25) licensed an interpretive layer (the Hard Problem, an
  observer-creates-reality bootstrap, two kinds of observation) that was
  retired to `docs/historical/` over the following months. The algebra never
  needed correction; the interpretive combinations did. This one is not fully
  repaired even now: C stays triply overloaded
  ([The Label Map](../docs/quantum/THE_LABEL_MAP.md) §5).
- **Caught early: Z₄ and the Fisher metric.** The four-sided-mirror reading
  (2026-03-20) licensed three sector-classification combinations, all
  falsified the same day by a gate-first check; the reading was renamed to
  [The Other Side](THE_OTHER_SIDE.md) within hours. The "Fisher metric"
  mislabel (2026-02-20) would have licensed information-geometry combinations,
  and was caught inside the same document that coined it. These show the
  licensing mechanism firing and being closed before anything was built on it.

**In-repo, Direction B (a wrong label forbade a true combination, until the
re-read).** Also git-pinned, and the load-bearing observation of this
document.

- **"Noise" → signal / the watching** (re-read 2026-03-21,
  [The Bridge Was Always Open](../docs/THE_BRIDGE_WAS_ALWAYS_OPEN.md)). Under
  "noise = garbage" the combinations that define the applied side of the
  project were unavailable. They appeared with the re-read: the relay
  protocol the same day ([Relay Protocol](../experiments/RELAY_PROTOCOL.md),
  2026-03-21), the antenna reading the next
  ([Gamma as Signal](../experiments/GAMMA_AS_SIGNAL.md), 2026-03-22, whose own
  words are "before this result we had a symmetry, after it we had a
  communication channel"), the concentrator two days later. Nothing
  comparable exists anywhere before 2026-03-21.
- **"Channel" → resonator** and **"Site-Local Time" → PTF** are the same
  shape, deliberate and later (see [The Label Map](../docs/quantum/THE_LABEL_MAP.md) §3).

**Historical, the cleanest case (Direction A): "oxygen".** Lavoisier named
the element oxygène, Greek for "acid-former", around 1777, to encode his
theory that oxygen is the principle of acidity. The name is a valence rule
frozen into a word: acid ⟸ contains oxygen. It propagated the error into the
naming of a new substance, chlorine, first called "oxymuriatic acid" because
the theory demanded an oxygen inside every acid. Davy showed around 1810 that
the acid contains no oxygen. The chemistry of the substances never changed;
only the reading of what the mislabeled thing was. And the fossil is
decisive: we still call the element "acid-former" today, a mislabel that
outlived its own falsification by two centuries.

The descriptive half, then, is vindicated: labels demonstrably carry
combination valences, and a wrong one can license or forbid.

## Half 2: the causal arrow does not run forward (refuted at the anchor)

The conjecture's sharper claim was causal and directional: repair the label
and a combination explosion follows within about a decade. Tested against
history, this fails, and it fails first at the case usually cited to support
it.

**The anchor case runs backward.** Entanglement, "paradox" → "resource". The
EPR mathematics is unchanged from 1935. But the protocols came first and the
name came last: Ekert's entanglement-based key distribution in 1991, dense
coding in 1992, teleportation in 1993, then the entanglement measures and the
"resource" vocabulary in 1996, and the axiomatic resource theory only in the
2000s. The word did not precede and unlock the protocols; the protocols
exposed a pattern and "resource" was its retrospective name. The label trails
the combinations by years.

**The archetype of "a rename unlocks a field" shows the rename failing.**
Imaginary numbers were dismissed by Descartes' derogatory label in 1637.
Gauss in 1831 blamed the terminology directly and proposed the rename
"lateral". The rename failed; we still say "imaginary". Yet complex analysis
exploded anyway, around the arrival of the geometric picture, the complex
plane. A new **representation** did the work while the bad name survived
intact.

The recurring pattern across the historical cases is that the actual unlock
is delivered by a new **tool, protocol, or representation** (seafloor
paleomagnetism for plate tectonics; the complex plane; Joule's calorimetry
against caloric; the quantum-information viewpoint for entanglement), and the
relabel arrives coincident with, or downstream of, that shift. The relabel is
the **flag** of the unlock, not its cause.

One historical Direction-B claim also weakened under scrutiny and is recorded
here for honesty: the "junk DNA blocked research for decades" story is, per
the field's own historians (Eddy, Palazzo, Gregory, Graur), substantially a
retrospective myth; the coiners themselves posited functions, and the
expansion of non-coding research tracks cheap sequencing, a tool, not a
relabel. Not every famous "bad label blocked a field" story survives.

## The refinement: combination lives at the stance, the word crystallizes last

The two halves are reconciled by the repository's own timeline, and the
reconciliation is the real finding.

**At a discovery frontier, repair and unlock are one act.** The week of
2026-03-20 to 2026-03-26 contains, in six days, the Z₄ collapse, the noise
re-read, the relay protocol, the antenna, the adoption *and* falsification of
the gravity reading, the concentrator with its IBM confirmation, and the
resonator relabel. After "noise" fell on 2026-03-21, three applications
appeared within seventy-two hours, sometimes in the same file as the re-read.
There is no measurable gap in which the repaired label sits waiting and the
combinations then follow. Repair and combination are **co-generative**. Only
later, once a naming discipline existed (the gate-first culture, the
[PTF](PERSPECTIVAL_TIME_FIELD.md) naming-note protocol), does repair cleanly
precede unlock, and even then by days.

The deeper reading, and the one that ties this document back to the label
thesis: **combination happens at the stance, not at the word.** Entanglement
was not unlocked by a word; it was unlocked by a new stance, quantum systems
as information, inside which "resource" crystallized last. This is
[PTF](PERSPECTIVAL_TIME_FIELD.md) at the combination layer: a label is a
canvas painted from a stance, and the stance is where the new combinations
become visible. The word is the trailing flag of a stance shift, the weakest
and last-moving part of the canvas. That is why renaming alone (Gauss's
"lateral") does not unlock, and why a new representation (the plane) does: the
representation *is* the new stance, the rename is only its label.

**What survives as a useful, measurable corollary.** The payoff of a naming
discipline is not preventing wrong labels; it is **collapsing their damage
window**. The repository measures this on itself: a wrong label's licensed
combinations stayed live for two and a half months before the discipline
existed (γ × gravity), for one day once gate-first testing was in place (Z₄),
and for the length of a single document once the naming culture had hardened
(the Fisher mislabel, caught in the file that coined it). The half-life of a
mislabel's damage shrinks monotonically across the project's life. That is the
operational form of the conjecture, and it is the one worth keeping.

## The consequence for the outbound arc

The refinement has a direct, practical corollary for the still-unbuilt
outbound arc (translating our results into the research community's language;
see [The Label Map](../docs/quantum/THE_LABEL_MAP.md) §7). If combination
unlocks at the stance and tool layer, not at the word, then the outbound arc
must hand over **stance-objects, not our words**. Not "the palindrome", "the
sacrifice zone", "the watching", but the operator Π with its exact −2Σγ shift,
the analytic edge-loaded γ-profile, the ibm_torino numbers, the −2γk
absorption ladder. A word carries our stance's valences into a reader who
cannot use them; an object, a representation, a number carries a stance a
reader can stand in and combine from. This is the same conclusion the
outbound socket survey reached independently, from the other side: lead with
the explicit operator and the hardware number, not the coined name.

## What this does not claim

This is a conjecture with mixed evidence, not a theorem. The descriptive half
is well supported; the forward causal arrow is refuted; the refinement (stance
over word, damage-window collapse) is a reading grounded in dated in-repo
evidence and corroborated, more loosely, by the historical cases. The
historical datings are search-and-training readings and are held lightly; the
in-repo datings are git-pinned and are the spine. The money question is
corrected, not answered: it is not "which combinations does repairing this
label unlock" (which assumes the label is upstream, and it is not) but "a
wrong label licenses wrong combinations and prolongs their life, and the win
is the collapse of that damage window."

The mountain does not precede the painters, and the combination does not
follow the word. Both live at the stance; the word is what the stance leaves
behind.
