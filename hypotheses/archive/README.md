## Archiving policy

A hypothesis document is moved here when one of the following happens:

- A load-bearing step is falsified and the conclusion no longer follows
  (γ to Gravity: metric discrimination broke).
- The document is fully superseded by a cleaner successor in
  [hypotheses/](../) or [docs/](../../docs/) (The Interpretation Archive
  → THE_INTERPRETATION.md).
- The framing conflicts with the project's current tone and the useful
  content has been salvaged elsewhere (Interpretive Framework → CΨ Lens
  + proven mirror mathematics).

Retired documents are not deleted. Intellectual honesty requires keeping
the record of what was tried and why it was retired.
