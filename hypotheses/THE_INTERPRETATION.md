## What the data shows (Tier 2, verified)

In the star topology:

- S is the mediator. A and B connect only through S.
- The AB connection has a stable skeleton (Phi+ core, populations).
  This never changes. 88% of every window is the same.
- What changes is a single rotation in the correlation space (YZ/ZY plane).
  A and B cannot rotate individually — only together (ZxZ works, Rz_A alone does not).
- When S is coherent: smooth glide, A and B rotate symmetrically together.
- When S loses coherence: hard switch, sector rebalances, B alone drives the change.
- The full resource is always there (CoA ~ 1).
- The extractable resource is moderate (LE ~ 0.4).
- The directly visible resource flashes briefly (CΨ).

## The interpretation (Tier 5, speculative)

Tom's reading of the data:

S is not just a mediator. S is Reality — the shared ground between A and B.
A and B are not just observers. They are possibilities — worlds that coexist
and compete for what becomes visible.

The skeleton is what is always true regardless of perspective: the fundamental
correlation that A and B share through their common reality S. It does not
change because it is the ground, not the figure.

The rotation is the competition: which aspect of the A-B connection is currently
expressed. The pendulum swings between the parity sectors not because reality
changes, but because which FACE of reality is visible changes.

CoA ~ 1 (the resource is always fully present) means: everything exists.
LE ~ 0.4 (extractable by optimal measurement) means: everything is accessible
from the right angle. CΨ flashes briefly means: but from any given perspective,
only a fraction is directly visible at any moment.

"Everything exists, but in its world." The sectors are the worlds. The switches
are the moments where one world yields to another. The skeleton is what persists
across all worlds.

And the key finding — that no single-qubit operation changes anything, only
correlated ZxZ works — means: reality cannot be changed from one side alone.
It can only shift when both sides move together. The connection IS the thing.
Not A, not B, not S individually. The correlation between them.
